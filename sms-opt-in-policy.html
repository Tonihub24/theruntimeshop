SMS Opt-In Policy

By providing your mobile number to RuntimeShop, you agree to receive SMS text messages from us related to your appointments, security alerts, and service updates.

These messages may include important reminders, technical notifications, or security verification codes. Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt-out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

RuntimeShop does not share your phone number with third parties. We value your privacy and only use SMS for legitimate customer purposes.
